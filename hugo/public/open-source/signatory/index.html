Signatory

Compliance doesn’t stop at architecture, it extends to how decisions and approvals are recorded. Signatory is an open-source platform for secure, auditable signing of compliance-related documents and declarations.

Signatory

Signatory provides a vendor-neutral, auditable system for collecting, timestamping, and verifying approvals on documents related to cloud governance, security, and regulatory compliance.

Built with institutional use in mind, Signatory ensures that key decisions, like policy approval, access certification, or incident response reviews, are formally recorded, tamper-evident, and verifiable.

Use Cases

  • Formal approval of cloud security and data protection policies
  • Access review sign-offs for SOC 2 / ISO 27001 control evidence
  • Incident resolution acknowledgement or RCA certification
  • Board or executive approval of regulatory submissions
  • Technical leadership sign-off on architecture risks

Why It Matters

Many compliance breakdowns come not from technology, but from the lack of traceable decisions. Signatory ensures your team can prove not only what was done, but who authorized it, when, and under what version of the document.
